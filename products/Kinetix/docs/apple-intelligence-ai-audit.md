## Migration Classification Summary
- **Phase-1 eligible (already in-scope):** post-run/readiness/pre-run/recovery text naturalization surfaces only.
- **Out of Phase-1 (documented only):** natural-language chat, backend AI chat/coach endpoint overhauls, memory/chat continuity redesign.
- **Never move to AI:** readiness/fatigue scoring math, recommendation decision logic, guardrails, and deterministic thresholds.
